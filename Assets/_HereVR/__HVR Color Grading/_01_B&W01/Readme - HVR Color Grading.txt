Thank you for buying the Asset.

Usage:

1. Add Unity Color Correction Lookup to your camera.
2. Choose a LUT from the pack.
3. If needed, adjust the LUT in 3rd party image editing app or use other collor correction methods (like Curves) for modifications.

Other Notes:

- Compatible with ColorFX form Asset Store what offers the possibility to change the strength of the effect on the fly with its LUT script.
